About

In my work I am concerned with questions of control and transformation. I am interested in the dynamic between the desire to determine the outcome of a process and the inability to decide upon certain results. Holding on to existing structures opposes change and transformation. Both on the individual and personal level, as well as on the societal level, we are subjects to change and transformation constantly. On both levels there is an ongoing conflict between forces that try to remain in existing realities, and forces that are willing to take risks in order to advance. Surrendering to this fact and the engagement in this reality form the core of my creative work.
